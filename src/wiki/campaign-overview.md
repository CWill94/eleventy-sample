The campaign begins around [[Billowbarrow]] and expands into wider conflicts tied to industrial magic, political unrest, and world-level threats.

- [[Oz]]

## Primary Arcs
- Fair Day escalation and collapse around oil/fracking activity
- Bullywug, gnome, and kobold conflict around slavery and weapons trade
- Recovery of major relics (including a red Bend of Maerlyn's Rainbow orb)
- Vallaki/Wachterhaus power struggle and Strahd-aligned cult activity
